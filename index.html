Why Hydration Matters

Proper hydration is essential for maintaining energy levels, cognitive function, and overall health. Yet, 75% of Americans are chronically dehydrated without even realizing it.

EcoBottle was created to solve this problem by making hydration tracking effortless and intuitive. With smart reminders and detailed analytics, you'll never forget to drink water again.

How It Works

  1. Fill & Track: Fill your EcoBottle and let the smart sensor automatically track your intake.
  2. Get Insights: View your hydration data in real-time on the bottle's display or in the app.
  3. Receive Reminders: Get gentle reminders when it's time to hydrate based on your personal needs.
  4. Achieve Goals: Meet your daily hydration targets and build healthier habits over time.
